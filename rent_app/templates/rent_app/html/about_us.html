Who We Are

Building a Better Rental Economy

RentHive is a peer-to-peer rental marketplace that connects people who have things with people who need them. Whether you're looking to rent electronics, furniture, or everyday items, we make the process seamless and secure.

Founded by Shiva Singh, we believe that ownership shouldn't be a prerequisite for access. Our platform empowers owners to monetize unused assets and helps renters save money while getting exactly what they need.
